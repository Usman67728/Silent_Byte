FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Silent Byte offer? +

We offer comprehensive cybersecurity solutions, custom web development, cloud architecture and migration, AI/ML implementation, security consulting, and 24/7 monitoring and support services.

How long does a typical project take? +

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Security audits typically take 2-4 weeks, web development projects range from 6-16 weeks, and cloud migrations depend on infrastructure size. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

Do you offer ongoing support? +

Yes! We offer various support tiers including 24/7 monitoring, dedicated account managers, priority incident response, and continuous security updates. Our Professional and Enterprise plans include comprehensive support packages.

What industries do you serve? +

We serve clients across all industries, with particular expertise in financial services, healthcare, e-commerce, technology startups, gaming, and government sectors. Our solutions are tailored to meet industry-specific compliance requirements.

How do I get started? +

Simply fill out the contact form above or give us a call. We'll schedule a free consultation to understand your needs, assess your current situation, and provide a customized proposal with clear pricing and timelines.
